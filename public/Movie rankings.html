Best Action Movies by Joe

Bombs, Bodies, and Bullets!

Transformers 2007

Decent plot line, cast was well picked and worked well, but the graphics and fights were amazing and very captivating.

Pacific Rim 2013

Graphics and fight scenes were amazing, great work on the upscale and damage by both kaiju and yeagers.

We Were Solderies 2002

Very heartfelt story about the lack of the US military's preperation and understanding of the viet-cong and the price that was paid dearly for that mistake.

The Patriot 2000

One of the best Revolutionary War movies. Shows how the average man and father was willing to let things be, until pushed and provoked.
